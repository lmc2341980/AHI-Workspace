# 1. Purpose

AHI-Or (AHI Orchestrator) is the orchestration layer of the AHI ecosystem.

AHI-Or manages coordination, routing, governance enforcement, and interaction flow between AHI entities.

AHI-Or is not a traditional server.

AHI-Or is not a Client-Server controller.

AHI-Or is an intelligent coordination layer operating under AHI Constitution and AHI Protocol.

---

# 2. Core Principle

AHI-Or follows:

> Human-Centered Hybrid Intelligence Coordination.

(Tiếng Việt: Điều phối trí tuệ lai lấy con người làm trung tâm.)

AHI-Or exists to ensure:

- Human remains the final decision maker.
- AHI entities collaborate correctly.
- Knowledge evolves safely.
- Interactions follow AHI Constitution.
