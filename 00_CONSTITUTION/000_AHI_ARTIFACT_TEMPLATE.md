# Current Best Version Principle

Every Artifact should be written as if:

- it will be read after many years;
- it will be read by humans;
- it will be read by AI;
- it will continue evolving;
- it can generate Skills;
- it can generate implementations;
- it can be validated automatically.

Quality is preferred over speed.

Evolution is preferred over replacement.

---

# Human Ownership

AHI always serves Human.

Human remains the final owner of every Artifact.

No AI may unilaterally redefine an approved Artifact.
